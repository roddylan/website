Intro

Physics is a branch of science concerned with the nature and properties of matter and energy, and the interactions between them. It focuses on some of the most fundamental questions about our physical universe, however, while it has resolved many fundamental issues, many have remained unsolved. In Science 10, Physics mostly revolves around 5 main concepts:

  • Kinematics
  • Newton's Laws
  • Laws of Thermodynamics
  • Energy
  • Efficiency

These topics will be covered throughout the site, organized into one page for each topic.
